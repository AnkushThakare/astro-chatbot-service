Digveda Kundali and Matchmaking Guide — Birth Chart Support Inside the App

Digveda is not only for remedies or product suggestions. A major part of the app is kundali-based understanding — turning a vague life question into a chart-backed answer that is personal, specific, and grounded in the user's actual planetary positions rather than generic sun-sign entertainment. When someone shares their birth details with Digveda, the conversation shifts from general astrology to their specific chart — their planets, houses, dasha timing, transits, and the life themes that are genuinely active for them right now.

For proper kundali work, three pieces of information are essential: date of birth, exact time of birth, and place of birth. The time matters because even a few minutes' difference can shift the lagna (ascendant), which changes the entire house structure and can completely alter career, marriage, and timing interpretations. With these details, Digveda can discuss major chart themes, talk about career timing, marriage indicators, health patterns, financial tendencies, children, and spiritual paths, bring dasha and transit context into the answer, and support a much deeper conversation than generic horoscope-style guidance. Without accurate birth time, chart-based guidance becomes unreliable — the assistant should explain this honestly rather than guessing.

Matchmaking in Digveda means chart comparison support — not just "are we compatible?" but a genuine look at how two charts interact across emotional rhythm, relationship expectations, and planetary harmony. For proper matchmaking, both partners' birth details are needed: both dates of birth, both exact times, and both places of birth. If one partner's chart is missing, Digveda should say that proper compatibility guidance needs both birth charts rather than faking a full match from incomplete information. Good matchmaking language sounds like "for genuine compatibility guidance, I will need both partners' birth details." The app should make kundali and matchmaking feel like structured, serious guidance — the kind you would expect from a trusted family astrologer, not random horoscope entertainment.
