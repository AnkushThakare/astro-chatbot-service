Nakshatras (Lunar Mansions) - Complete Guide

The 27 nakshatras divide the 360-degree zodiac into 13.33-degree segments. Each nakshatra has a ruling planet, deity, and unique personality. The Moon's nakshatra at birth determines the starting Vimshottari dasha and deeply influences personality.

Ketu-ruled Nakshatras (spiritual, past-karma oriented):

Ashwini (0-13.20 Aries)
Deity: Ashwini Kumaras (divine healers). Quick, energetic, natural healers. Good in medicine, emergency services, sports. Impulsive in love but loyal. Compatible with Bharani, Pushya, Ashlesha.

Magha (0-13.20 Leo)
Deity: Pitris (ancestors). Royal bearing, respects tradition, authority-driven. Good in politics, administration, family business. Values lineage and status in relationships. Compatible with Purva Phalguni, Uttara Phalguni.

Moola (0-13.20 Sagittarius)
Deity: Nirriti (goddess of dissolution). Investigative, research-oriented, gets to the root of things. Good in research, medicine, detective work. Relationships undergo transformations. Compatible with Ashwini, Ardra.

Venus-ruled Nakshatras (luxury, creativity, relationships):

Bharani (13.20-26.40 Aries)
Deity: Yama (god of death/dharma). Intense, creative, bears heavy responsibility. Good in law, creative arts, hospitality. Deep emotional bonds. Compatible with Ashwini, Rohini, Pushya.

Purva Phalguni (13.20-26.40 Leo)
Deity: Bhaga (god of fortune). Charismatic, artistic, loves luxury. Good in entertainment, hospitality, counseling. Romantic, pleasure-seeking in love. Compatible with Magha, Uttara Phalguni.

Purva Ashadha (13.20-26.40 Sagittarius)
Deity: Apas (water deity). Persuasive, confident, invincible spirit. Good in media, law, motivation. Idealistic in relationships. Compatible with Uttara Ashadha, Moola.

Sun-ruled Nakshatras (authority, confidence, leadership):

Krittika (26.40 Aries - 10.00 Taurus)
Deity: Agni (fire god). Sharp, critical, purifying nature. Good in military, cooking, criticism, editing. Direct and honest in relationships. Compatible with Rohini, Mrigashira.

Uttara Phalguni (26.40 Leo - 10.00 Virgo)
Deity: Aryaman (god of patronage). Generous, helpful, friendly. Good in social work, counseling, HR. Stable, loyal partnerships. Compatible with Purva Phalguni, Hasta.

Uttara Ashadha (26.40 Sagittarius - 10.00 Capricorn)
Deity: Vishvadevas (universal gods). Principled, ambitious, eventual success. Good in government, leadership, long-term projects. Committed relationships. Compatible with Purva Ashadha, Shravana.

Moon-ruled Nakshatras (nurturing, emotional, public-facing):

Rohini (10.00-23.20 Taurus)
Deity: Brahma (creator). Beautiful, creative, magnetic. Good in arts, beauty, agriculture, fashion. Very romantic, can be possessive. Compatible with Mrigashira, Krittika.

Hasta (10.00-23.20 Virgo)
Deity: Savitar (sun god). Skilled with hands, clever, resourceful. Good in crafts, healing, magic, technology. Adaptable in relationships. Compatible with Uttara Phalguni, Chitra.

Shravana (10.00-23.20 Capricorn)
Deity: Vishnu (preserver). Good listener, knowledge-seeker, organizational skills. Good in education, counseling, media. Values communication in relationships. Compatible with Uttara Ashadha, Dhanishta.

Mars-ruled Nakshatras (energy, courage, action):

Mrigashira (23.20 Taurus - 6.40 Gemini)
Deity: Soma (moon god). Curious, searching, gentle warrior. Good in research, travel, marketing. Seeks the perfect partner, restless in love. Compatible with Rohini, Ardra.

Chitra (23.20 Virgo - 6.40 Libra)
Deity: Tvashtar (celestial architect). Creative, artistic, visually oriented. Good in architecture, design, fashion, gems. Attracted to beauty. Compatible with Hasta, Swati.

Dhanishta (23.20 Capricorn - 6.40 Aquarius)
Deity: Vasus (elemental gods). Musical, wealthy, ambitious. Good in music, real estate, sports. Can be possessive. Compatible with Shravana, Shatabhisha.

Rahu-ruled Nakshatras (unconventional, foreign, transformative):

Ardra (6.40-20.00 Gemini)
Deity: Rudra (storm god). Intense, intellectual, goes through storms. Good in research, technology, pharmaceuticals. Relationships have ups and downs. Compatible with Punarvasu, Mrigashira.

Swati (6.40-20.00 Libra)
Deity: Vayu (wind god). Independent, diplomatic, flexible. Good in trade, business, travel. Values freedom in relationships. Compatible with Chitra, Vishakha.

Shatabhisha (6.40-20.00 Aquarius)
Deity: Varuna (cosmic waters). Mysterious, healing, secretive. Good in alternative medicine, astrology, technology. Private in relationships. Compatible with Dhanishta, Purva Bhadrapada.

Jupiter-ruled Nakshatras (wisdom, expansion, spirituality):

Punarvasu (20.00 Gemini - 3.20 Cancer)
Deity: Aditi (mother of gods). Optimistic, philosophical, returns to goodness. Good in teaching, travel, writing. Nurturing partner. Compatible with Pushya, Ardra.

Vishakha (20.00 Libra - 3.20 Scorpio)
Deity: Indra-Agni. Determined, goal-oriented, split focus. Good in business, politics, competition. Intense in relationships. Compatible with Swati, Anuradha.

Purva Bhadrapada (20.00 Aquarius - 3.20 Pisces)
Deity: Aja Ekapada. Passionate, extreme, transformative. Good in occult, research, social change. Intense emotional connections. Compatible with Shatabhisha, Uttara Bhadrapada.

Saturn-ruled Nakshatras (discipline, endurance, service):

Pushya (3.20-16.40 Cancer)
Deity: Brihaspati (Jupiter). Most auspicious nakshatra. Nourishing, wise, selfless. Good in education, counseling, caregiving. Devoted partner. Compatible with Punarvasu, Ashlesha.

Anuradha (3.20-16.40 Scorpio)
Deity: Mitra (god of friendship). Loyal, disciplined, loves deeply. Good in organizations, research, foreign connections. Devoted but demanding. Compatible with Vishakha, Jyeshtha.

Uttara Bhadrapada (3.20-16.40 Pisces)
Deity: Ahir Budhnya (serpent of depths). Deep, wise, spiritually inclined. Good in counseling, charity, spiritual teaching. Seeks meaningful connections. Compatible with Purva Bhadrapada, Revati.

Mercury-ruled Nakshatras (intellect, communication, adaptability):

Ashlesha (16.40-30.00 Cancer)
Deity: Nagas (serpent gods). Perceptive, mystical, hypnotic. Good in psychology, research, politics. Complex in relationships. Compatible with Pushya, Magha.

Jyeshtha (16.40-30.00 Scorpio)
Deity: Indra (king of gods). Protective, senior, responsible. Good in leadership, military, administration. Protective of partner. Compatible with Anuradha, Moola.

Revati (16.40-30.00 Pisces)
Deity: Pushan (nourisher). Gentle, creative, spiritual. Good in arts, travel, animal care. Compassionate in relationships. Compatible with Uttara Bhadrapada, Ashwini.

Nakshatra Compatibility in Matchmaking
Nakshatras sharing the same ruling planet generally have good mutual understanding. The Tara (star compatibility) score in guna milan checks how two birth nakshatras interact. Nakshatras 3, 5, 7 positions from each other are favorable; 2, 4, 6 can indicate tension.
