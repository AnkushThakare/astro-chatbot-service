Planetary Remedies Through Rudraksha and Bracelets

When specific planets are weak or afflicted in a person's birth chart (kundali), wearing the right rudraksha or bracelet can help balance the planetary energies. This guide maps common astrological concerns to appropriate product remedies from the Digveda catalog.

Sun (Surya) - Weak or Afflicted
Symptoms: Low confidence, poor eyesight, issues with authority figures, father-related problems
Remedy: 1 Mukhi or 12 Mukhi Rudraksha
Bracelet: Solar energy bracelet with rudraksha beads
Day to start wearing: Sunday
Mantra: Om Suryaya Namah

Moon (Chandra) - Weak or Afflicted
Symptoms: Emotional instability, anxiety, sleep issues, mother-related problems, mental restlessness
Remedy: 2 Mukhi Rudraksha
Bracelet: Calming energy bracelet
Day to start wearing: Monday
Mantra: Om Chandraya Namah

Mars (Mangal) - Weak or Afflicted / Mangal Dosha
Symptoms: Anger issues, low energy, accidents, property disputes, delayed marriage (manglik)
Remedy: 3 Mukhi Rudraksha
Bracelet: Mars protection bracelet
Day to start wearing: Tuesday
Mantra: Om Mangalaya Namah

Mercury (Budh) - Weak or Afflicted
Symptoms: Communication problems, poor memory, skin issues, business losses
Remedy: 4 Mukhi Rudraksha
Bracelet: Focus and clarity bracelet
Day to start wearing: Wednesday
Mantra: Om Budhaya Namah

Jupiter (Guru) - Weak or Afflicted
Symptoms: Lack of wisdom, financial instability, issues with teachers/mentors, delayed children
Remedy: 5 Mukhi Rudraksha (most accessible), 5 Mukhi Mala for japa
Bracelet: Wisdom and prosperity bracelet
Day to start wearing: Thursday
Mantra: Om Gurave Namah

Venus (Shukra) - Weak or Afflicted
Symptoms: Relationship troubles, lack of luxury, artistic blocks, reproductive health issues
Remedy: 6 Mukhi Rudraksha
Bracelet: Love and harmony bracelet
Day to start wearing: Friday
Mantra: Om Shukraya Namah

Saturn (Shani) - Weak or Afflicted / Sade Sati / Shani Dhaiya
Symptoms: Delays, obstacles, chronic health issues, career stagnation, legal troubles
Remedy: 7 Mukhi Rudraksha or 14 Mukhi Rudraksha
Bracelet: Saturn protection bracelet
Day to start wearing: Saturday
Mantra: Om Shanaischaraya Namah
Note: Saturn periods (Sade Sati, Shani Dhaiya) are the most common reason users seek remedies

Rahu - Afflicted

Bracelet: Rahu protection bracelet

Ketu - Afflicted

General Protection
For users who are unsure of their specific planetary issues or want overall protection:
- 5 Mukhi Rudraksha Mala (108 beads) - suitable for everyone
- Navagraha bracelet - addresses all nine planetary energies
- Panchmukhi (5 face) rudraksha pendant - most versatile single bead

Important Disclaimer
Rudraksha and bracelets are supportive spiritual tools. They complement but do not replace:
- Professional medical advice for health concerns
- Professional counseling for relationship or mental health issues
- Practical action for career and financial goals
- Detailed kundali analysis by a qualified astrologer

Always recommend consulting with a pandit for personalized guidance based on the user's specific birth chart analysis.
