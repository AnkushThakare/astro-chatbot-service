Rudraksha Guide - Types, Benefits, and Planetary Associations

Rudraksha beads are sacred seeds of the Elaeocarpus ganitrus tree, revered in Vedic tradition for their spiritual and astrological significance. Each type of rudraksha is identified by the number of mukhis (faces or clefts) on its surface.

1 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Sun (Surya)
- Ruling deity: Lord Shiva
- Benefits: Leadership, self-confidence, clarity of purpose, spiritual awakening
- Best for: People seeking moksha, those with weak Sun in their chart
- Rarest and most expensive variety

2 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Moon (Chandra)
- Ruling deity: Ardhanarishwara
- Benefits: Emotional balance, harmony in relationships, peace of mind
- Best for: Moon-related afflictions, relationship issues, emotional instability

3 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Mars (Mangal)
- Ruling deity: Agni
- Benefits: Courage, vitality, self-confidence, overcoming lethargy
- Best for: Mangal dosha, low energy, lack of motivation, anger management

4 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Mercury (Budh)
- Ruling deity: Lord Brahma
- Benefits: Knowledge, communication, creativity, analytical thinking
- Best for: Students, professionals, those with weak Mercury

5 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Jupiter (Guru/Brihaspati)
- Ruling deity: Lord Kalagni Rudra
- Benefits: General well-being, health, peace, wisdom
- Best for: Daily wear, most commonly available, suitable for everyone
- Most affordable and widely recommended as a starting rudraksha

6 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Venus (Shukra)
- Ruling deity: Lord Kartikeya
- Benefits: Love, attraction, artistic expression, material comfort
- Best for: Relationship harmony, creative pursuits, Venus-related issues

7 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Saturn (Shani)
- Ruling deity: Goddess Mahalakshmi
- Benefits: Wealth, financial stability, patience, discipline
- Best for: Saturn-related challenges (Sade Sati, Shani Dhaiya), financial problems

8 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Rahu
- Ruling deity: Lord Ganesha
- Benefits: Obstacle removal, protection from negative energies, success in ventures
- Best for: Rahu-related issues, unexpected obstacles, kaal sarp dosha

9 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Ketu
- Ruling deity: Goddess Durga
- Benefits: Spiritual growth, protection, fearlessness, karma resolution
- Best for: Ketu-related issues, past-life karma, spiritual seekers

10 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: All nine planets (Navagraha)
- Ruling deity: Lord Vishnu
- Benefits: Protection from all planetary afflictions, overall harmony
- Best for: General planetary pacification, those with multiple planetary issues

11 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: All planets
- Ruling deity: Lord Hanuman
- Benefits: Courage, protection during travel, fearlessness, spiritual power
- Best for: Those seeking protection, travelers, people facing legal issues

14 Mukhi Rudraksha
- Ruling planet: Saturn
- Ruling deity: Lord Hanuman
- Benefits: Intuition, divine protection, third-eye activation
- Best for: Spiritual practitioners, astrologers, those seeking higher consciousness

How to Wear Rudraksha
- Energize (prana pratishtha) before wearing
- Wear on Monday or during auspicious muhurat
- Can be worn as mala (108 beads), bracelet, or pendant
- Touch your skin for maximum benefit
- Remove during non-vegetarian meals and alcohol consumption (traditional guidance)
- Chant the associated mantra while wearing

Rudraksha Mala
- Traditional 108+1 bead mala for japa meditation
- 5 mukhi mala is most common for daily practice
- Can combine different mukhi beads for specific purposes
- Gauri Shankar rudraksha (naturally joined pair) is considered highly auspicious for couples

Care Instructions
- Clean with water periodically
- Oil with mustard or olive oil monthly
- Avoid chemical exposure
- Store in a clean, sacred space when not wearing
