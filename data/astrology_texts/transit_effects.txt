Planetary Transit Effects - Detailed Guide

Transits (Gochar) show where planets are NOW compared to your birth chart. Saturn and Jupiter transits are most impactful because they move slowly and stay in each sign for extended periods.

Saturn Transit Through Houses (from Moon sign)

Saturn transits each sign for approximately 2.5 years. It is the most feared and most transformative transit.

Saturn in 1st house from Moon (Sade Sati peak): The most intense phase. Health challenges, mental pressure, identity transformation. Not always negative — can bring discipline and maturity. Results depend on Saturn's natal strength. Remedy: 7 mukhi rudraksha, Hanuman Chalisa daily.

Saturn in 2nd house from Moon (Sade Sati ending): Financial pressure easing, family matters settling. Speech may have been restricted. Savings start rebuilding. The lessons of Sade Sati begin consolidating.

Saturn in 3rd house from Moon: One of the best Saturn transits. Courage increases, short travels succeed, communication improves. Siblings benefit. Good time for writing, media, entrepreneurship.

Saturn in 4th house from Moon (Kantaka Shani / Dhaiya): Domestic pressure, mother's health concern, property disputes, emotional heaviness. Home life feels restricted. Vehicle issues possible. Remedy: Shani mantra, service to elderly.

Saturn in 5th house from Moon: Challenges with children, education stress, creative blocks. Romance may suffer delays. Speculation should be avoided. Good for disciplined study.

Saturn in 6th house from Moon: Excellent transit. Victory over enemies, health improves, debts clear, daily work becomes productive. One of the best positions for Saturn. Legal matters favor you.

Saturn in 7th house from Moon: Relationship tests, partnership restructuring. Marriage delays or pressure. Business partnerships need renegotiation. Patience in all partnerships is essential.

Saturn in 8th house from Moon (Ashtama Shani): Second most challenging transit after Sade Sati. Sudden obstacles, health scares, hidden problems surface. Inheritance matters. Transformation is deep but painful. Remedy: 14 mukhi rudraksha, Mahamrityunjaya mantra.

Saturn in 9th house from Moon: Father's health concern, spiritual questioning, long travel may face obstacles. Luck feels reduced. Good for deepening spiritual practice and questioning beliefs.

Saturn in 10th house from Moon: Career pressure and restructuring. Authority figures create challenges. May change jobs or roles. Hard work gets rewarded eventually. Good for building long-term career foundation.

Saturn in 11th house from Moon: Excellent transit. Income increases, goals materialize, social network expands. One of the best positions. Long-term investments pay off. Friendships deepen.

Saturn in 12th house from Moon (Sade Sati beginning): Expenses increase, sleep may be disturbed, foreign travel possible. The beginning of Sade Sati — subtle changes start. Spirituality draws attention. Remedy: Start Shani practices preventively.

Sade Sati - Detailed Explanation

Sade Sati is Saturn's 7.5-year transit through the 12th, 1st, and 2nd houses from natal Moon. It occurs roughly every 29.5 years.

Phase 1 (12th from Moon, ~2.5 years): Rising phase. Expenses increase, sleep disruptions, vague unease. Changes are subtle. Foreign travel or relocation possible. Spirituality beckons.

Phase 2 (1st from Moon, ~2.5 years): Peak phase. Maximum pressure on mind and body. Identity transformation. What no longer serves you falls away. This is the hardest but also the most growth-producing phase.

Phase 3 (2nd from Moon, ~2.5 years): Setting phase. Financial pressure, family adjustments. Speech may be constrained. The worst is over and lessons start integrating.

Common Misconceptions About Sade Sati:
- It is NOT always bad. Many people achieve their greatest success during Sade Sati.
- If Saturn is well-placed natally (exalted, own sign, yogakaraka), Sade Sati can actually be positive.
- The effects depend heavily on the Moon sign and Saturn's relationship with Moon.
- Sade Sati for Taurus, Libra, Capricorn, and Aquarius Moon signs is often milder.
- Sade Sati for Cancer and Leo Moon signs tends to be more challenging.

Jupiter Transit Through Houses (from Moon sign)

Jupiter transits each sign for approximately 1 year. Generally benefic, Jupiter expands whatever house it touches.

Jupiter in 1st from Moon: Excellent. Personal growth, optimism, health improvement. New beginnings favored. Weight gain possible.
Jupiter in 2nd from Moon: Wealth increase, family harmony, speech improves. Good for savings and investments.
Jupiter in 3rd from Moon: Mixed. Efforts increase but results may lag. Short travel. Sibling matters.
Jupiter in 4th from Moon: Mother's blessings, property gains, domestic happiness. Education succeeds. Inner peace.
Jupiter in 5th from Moon: Excellent for children, romance, creativity, education. One of the best transits. Speculation may succeed.
Jupiter in 6th from Moon: Health improvement, victory over competitors, debt clearance. Service-oriented work succeeds.
Jupiter in 7th from Moon: Marriage, partnerships flourish. Business expansion. Relationship harmony. Travel with partner.
Jupiter in 8th from Moon: Challenging. Health concerns, obstacles. But good for research, occult, inheritance. Transformation through wisdom.
Jupiter in 9th from Moon: Superb. Luck, higher learning, long travel, father's blessings, spiritual growth. One of the best positions.
Jupiter in 10th from Moon: Career peak. Recognition, promotions, authority. Public image improves. Hard work pays off.
Jupiter in 11th from Moon: Best transit of all. Income surge, goals achieved, social expansion. Wish fulfillment.
Jupiter in 12th from Moon: Expenses increase but on good things (charity, spiritual travel). Foreign connections. Moksha-oriented.

Rahu-Ketu Transit

Rahu and Ketu change signs approximately every 18 months. They always move in opposite directions (retrograde).

Rahu transiting 1st house: Identity confusion, unconventional desires, foreign connections. Can bring sudden opportunity or obsessive behavior.
Rahu in 7th house: Unusual relationship dynamics, cross-cultural partnerships, business with foreigners.
Rahu in 10th house: Ambitious career moves, unconventional success, media/technology roles.
Ketu in 1st house: Spiritual awakening, detachment from ego, past-life connections surfacing.
Ketu in 4th house: Domestic changes, mother concerns, property matters. Inner restlessness.
Ketu in 12th house: Excellent for spiritual growth, meditation, moksha. Foreign residence possible.

How to Use Transit Information
1. Check Saturn and Jupiter transits first — they have the most lasting impact
2. The house from Moon sign is the primary reference point
3. Also check house from Ascendant for a second layer of interpretation
4. Favorable transits amplify good natal placements; challenging transits test weak ones
5. Remedies work best when started before or at the beginning of a difficult transit
